Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is an Italian dish that is mostly served with pasta and tomato sauce.

It's easy to make and won't take much time, and it's so good!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Salt the chicken and use pepper.
  2. Put the chicken in the egg and then dip it in the flour, then egg again.
  3. Coat the chicken in the breadcrumbs.
  4. Pan fry them for 2 minutes per side, then put mozzarella on top and put it in the oven for 5 minutes.
