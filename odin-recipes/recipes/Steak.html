Steak

Description

Steak is a great recipe because it contains a lot of healthy fat and protein, and it is also very good.

Steak is the type of food that is better when you share it with your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • Any cut of steak you like
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Salt heavily the steak and put pepper on it as well.
  2. Get a pan and make it super hot to develop a good crust.
  3. Put your steak in the pan and turn it every 2 minutes.
  4. After 6 minutes, put the heat on medium and add the butter. Baste your steak for 30 seconds.
  5. After that, put your steak on a plate and let it rest for 10 minutes. Enjoy!
